To eliminate the amount of the chemistry in the diet is nowadays not only a trend but also a necessity in the process of the selection and purchase of products for consumption We are aware of the harmfulness of pesticides and chemical sprayings from agro-commodities to our organism and its other consequences, as well as the excessive burden on soil and its surroundings during cultivation and production.
DH Landscape Invest was established as the first of the companies in 2004 to logically follow the family tradition of farmers founded in 1925. The company merges several “green” projects whose primary concept is organic farming. Growing crops organically means reducing the use of nitrogen fertilizers, without acidifying soils, water resources, less greenhouse gas emissions and creating conditions for biodiversity of insects and local ecosystems.
The primary projects include research into new processes in the cultivation of grasses and multiannual fodder in an organic way and their application in practice, thereby promoting more environmentally friendly practices in agriculture. We develop mixes specializing in breeding horses and other livestock, where we focus on both nutritional values and a gentle production path that does not burden the local natural environment.
We are also engaged in retailing and wholesaling of agricultural commodities such as fodder grasses and feed grain cereals.
In addition to growing non-chemical crops, we are engaged in growing organic vegetables and fruits without the use of chemical fertilizers and spraying.
