Nowadays, the investment process is a common practice how to passively make a profit by using your funds.
The founder of DH Finance, in his early days of investing in the financial markets, has worked and managed his investments in this sector through established companies such as Across Private Investments, Tatra Banka Wealth Management or Slavia Capital. Later, as a part of the reorganization of the portfolio, there was a need to set up an independent company that continued to invest in the financial markets with larger assets at home and abroad.
Today, DH Finance's business consists of collecting and analyzing information regarding the development of financial markets, current investment opportunities, the use and performance of investment instruments, risk assessment and the selection of the right investment strategies.
Private equity transactions and fixed income investments are a major part of the company's product portfolio.
Trading in the equity, share and commodity markets has its equal part in our portfolio. Later on, we became a partner of Tatra Banka as a franchise partner in the Raiffeisen Bank Slovakia project.
